About Police Station Agent

Specialist Police Station Representative Agency

Expert criminal defence representation at Kent police stations, providing qualified solicitors and accredited representatives at short notice

Who We Are

Police Station Agent is a specialist agency providing qualified criminal solicitors and accredited police station representatives to assist clients during police interviews under caution across Kent.

Through our professional association with Tuckers Solicitors LLP, we represent members of the public free of charge at police interviews under caution, whether at a police station or elsewhere, where they are suspected of a criminal offence.

We also provide reliable agent cover services to criminal law firms, delivering professional representation that maintains their client relationships.

Our Expertise & Experience

Qualified Professionals Only

We employ only fully qualified criminal solicitors or fully qualified police station representatives. Our team has many years of criminal casework experience, and our solicitors regularly appear in Kent courts.

Every Type of Criminal Offence

We handle every type of offence without exception: public order to murder, domestic assaults to rape, terrorism to fraud, and everything in between. No allegation is too serious or too minor for expert representation.

Police Station Specialists

We exclusively represent members of the public suspected of committing criminal offences. We cover voluntary interviews by police free of charge under the police station advice and assistance scheme - these "attend by appointment" interviews are just as critical as custody interviews.

Comprehensive Coverage

We cover both Legal Aid and private work, plus regulatory matters for criminal practices. Our extended hours service means we're available when you need us - evenings, weekends, and bank holidays across all Kent police stations.

Led by Robert Cashman

Former Practice Director, 35 Years + 6000 + Cases

Robert Cashman is a qualified solicitor with over 35 years of criminal law experience handling 6000 + cases. As a former director of a major criminal legal aid practice, he brings director-level expertise and insight to every case.

An accredited Police Station Representative and Duty Solicitor, Robert is also a Higher Court Advocate (Criminal) qualified to appear in the Crown Court. He is a member of the Law Society and specializes in police station advice and representation.

Learn more about Higher Court Advocates: For an in-depth understanding of the role and expertise of Higher Court Advocates in the UK legal system, read our detailed guide on Higher Court Advocates.

Through Defence Legal Services, Robert maintains a professional association with Tuckers Solicitors LLP and is a Legal Aid Agency contracted provider offering agent cover for law firms across Kent and beyond.

Robert Cashman is a criminal defence solicitor. All legal services are provided through Tuckers Solicitors, which is authorised and regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority (SRA ID: 127795).
